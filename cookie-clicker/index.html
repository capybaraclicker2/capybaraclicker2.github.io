How to Play Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is the ultimate browser-based baking adventure. Tap to bake as many chocolate chip cookies as possible and build a global baking monopoly!

  • Bake: Tap the large chocolate chip cookie on the screen to instantly bake cookies!
  • Hire Help: Purchase cursors and kind grandmas to bake cookies automatically in the background.
  • Dynamic Skins: Every click cycles through a variety of delicious, high-quality chocolate chip cookie sticker designs!

Baker's Strategy Tip!

Balance your investments! Early on, active click power from Super Clickers gives a huge boost, but scaling up with Grandmas and Cookie Factories builds an unstoppable automated cookie empire.

Your bakery is saved automatically in your browser's local storage, meaning you can resume your empire anytime!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my progress saved offline?

Yes! Your progress is stored locally in your web browser. You can close the tab or reboot your device, and your cookies will still be waiting for you.

What is the ultimate upgrade?

The Cookie Factory is currently the highest-tier bakery. Accumulating factories will allow you to generate millions of cookies in no time.
